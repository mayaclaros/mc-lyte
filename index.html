Why is MC Lyte So Dope?

Lana Moorer or professionally known as MC LYTE is the FIRST female to perform hip hop in the White House and FIRST African American female to serve as the President of the Los Angeles chapter of the Recording Academy (Grammy Organization). MC LYTE is a lyricist, inspirational speaker, dj, pioneer, icon, veteran , entrepreneur and one of the most prolific and well-respected female Hip Hop artists of all time.
